Welcome to Naga City

Discover the heart of Bicol—where culture, adventure, history, and flavor meet. Naga City offers a unique blend of modern vibrance and rich heritage, making it one of the top travel destinations in the Philippines.

Naga Metropolitan Cathedral

The Naga Metropolitan Cathedral, also known as the Basilica Minore de Nuestra Señora de Peñafrancia, is an iconic religious structure located in Naga City, Philippines. This majestic cathedral serves not just as a place of worship but also as a cultural and historical symbol for the local community.

Architecturally, the cathedral features a blend of neo-Gothic and modern design elements. Its vibrant blue and white facade, complemented by intricately designed stained-glass windows, draws the eyes of passersby. Inside, the cathedral is spacious and decorated with religious art, creating a serene atmosphere conducive to prayer and reflection.

Peñafrancia Basilica Minore

Peñafrancia Basilica Minore, located in Naga City, is a significant religious site and a centerpiece of devotion for many Catholics in the region. Dedicated to Our Lady of Peñafrancia, it attracts thousands of pilgrims each year during the Peñafrancia Festival.

Plaza Rizal

Plaza Rizal, situated in the heart of Naga City, is a vibrant public square honoring Dr. José Rizal. It combines cultural significance with recreational space and lush landscaping.


Naga City People’s Mall

Naga City People’s Mall is a vibrant shopping destination located in the heart of Naga City, Philippines. As a modern retail facility, it offers a variety of shopping, dining, and entertainment options, making it a popular spot for both locals and tourists alike. Spanning multiple floors, the mall features numerous stores, from local brands to international retailers, providing an extensive array of products.


Our Lady of Peñafrancia Shrine

The Our Lady of Peñafrancia Shrine, located in Naga City, Philippines, is a revered religious site dedicated to the Virgin Mary, who is venerated as the Patroness of Bicolandia. The shrine serves as a focal point for numerous devotees who come to pay homage and seek blessings. This remarkable shrine stands as a testament to the deep religious roots and rich cultural heritage of the Filipino people.


Kamarikutan Café and Gallery

Kamarikutan Café and Gallery, located in the heart of Naga City in the Philippines, is a vibrant hub that beautifully combines art, culture, and culinary delights. This unique establishment offers a cozy atmosphere where visitors can enjoy delicious local coffee and food while immersing themselves in a variety of artistic expressions. Kamarikutan, which translates to "to blend" in the local dialect, epitomizes the fusion of gastronomic flavors and creative artistry.


Basilica Minore de Nuestra Señora de Peñafrancia

The Basilica Minore de Nuestra Señora de Peñafrancia, located in Naga City, Philippines, is a significant religious site and a revered pilgrimage destination. This basilica is dedicated to the Virgin of Peñafrancia, also known as the Patroness of Bicolandia. Renowned for its stunning architecture and rich spiritual ambiance, the basilica draws both locals and tourists who seek to marvel at its beauty and connect with their faith.


San Francisco Church

San Francisco Church, located in Naga City, Philippines, is a stunning example of colonial architecture that showcases the rich cultural and religious heritage of the region. This beautiful church, dedicated to St. Francis of Assisi, serves as a spiritual haven for the local community and a captivating landmark for visitors.


Naga City Eco-Tourism Park

Naga City Eco-Tourism Park is a stunning destination located in the heart of Naga, Philippines. This park is a testament to the country's commitment to preserving its natural beauty while promoting eco-friendly tourism. Spanning across lush greenery, it offers a unique blend of outdoor adventure and relaxation, making it a perfect getaway for both locals and tourists.


Camsur Watersports Complex

The Camsur Watersports Complex, often referred to as CWC, is a premier water sports destination located in Naga City, Philippines. Situated within the stunning backdrop of the Bicol region, this facility has become a hub for both water sports enthusiasts and casual visitors alike. Established in 2006, the complex offers state-of-the-art amenities, including a complex cable ski system, numerous wakeboarding spots, and facilities designed for various water sports activities.
